Monster Survivors

Survive against endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure!

How to Play

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Attack: Automatic
  • Choose Upgrades: Mouse Click
  • Pause Game: ESC

Objective:

Survive as long as possible against waves of monsters. Collect experience points to level up and choose powerful upgrades to enhance your character's abilities.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival action game where you face endless waves of monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating as many monsters as you can.

As you progress through the game, you'll face increasingly difficult enemies and bosses. Each defeated monster drops experience points that allow you to level up and choose from various upgrades, weapons, and abilities to enhance your character.

The game features a dynamic difficulty system that ensures each playthrough is challenging and unique. With randomized enemy spawns and upgrade options, no two games will ever be the same!

Game Features

Character Progression

Level up your character and choose from a variety of upgrades to create unique builds. Experiment with different combinations of weapons and passive abilities to find the most effective strategy.

Monster Variety

Face off against dozens of different monster types, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns. From basic minions to challenging elite monsters and epic bosses!

Dynamic Environments

Explore various environments that change as you progress through the game. Each area presents new challenges and opportunities for strategic gameplay.

Leaderboards

Compete with players from around the world to achieve the highest score and longest survival time. Can you make it to the top of the leaderboard?

Tips & Strategies

  • •

    Movement is key: Never stop moving and always keep a safe distance from enemy groups.

  • •

    Focus on a build: Choose upgrades that complement each other rather than picking random abilities.

  • •

    Prioritize area damage: Weapons that can hit multiple enemies at once are often more valuable than single-target weapons.

  • •

    Collect all experience: Make sure to pick up all experience orbs dropped by defeated monsters to level up faster.

  • •

    Watch for treasure chests: These can contain valuable items that provide significant advantages.
